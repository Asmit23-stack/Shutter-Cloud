Frequent Asked Question

What is Shutter Cloud ?

Shutter Cloud is a cloud storage and photo sharing platform designed specifically for photographers. It offers secure storage, easy sharing options, and tools tailored for managing and showcasing your photography portfolio.

How much does Shutter Storage code ?

Shutter Storage at just Rs 1/GB per year.

How does Shutter Finder work ?

Shutter Finder allows event photographers to upload and share photos with attendees using QR codes. After an event, photographers can upload their photos to a Shutter Finder album and generate a unique QR code. Attendees can then scan this code to access and download their photos.

Is my data secure with Shutter Cloud ?

Yes, we use multiple backups and advanced security measures to protect your data. This includes end-to-end encryption, regular security audits, and compliance with industry-standard data protection regulations. Your photos and personal information are always kept private and secure.

What file formats does Shutter Cloud support ?

Shutter Cloud supports a wide range of image formats including JPEG, PNG, RAW files from major camera manufacturers, TIFF, and more. We also support video files, making it a comprehensive solution for all your visual content.

How can I access my photos on Shutter Cloud ?

You can access your photos on Shutter Cloud through our web interface on any browser. This ensures you have access to your entire photo library wherever you go.
